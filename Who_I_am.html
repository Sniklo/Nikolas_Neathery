About me

I am a student at Worcester Polytechnic Institute studying Robotics Engineering. I am in my fourth year of study and will be graduating in May of 2023. My passion is to constantly learn new skills even if they dont pertain to my major or current job.

Passion for making things

Going all the way back to my days in middle school I've always had a passion for making things. Whether it be gifts for my parents or a FRC robot that would go on to be a district champion and world finalist. Every position I've held in my career has had some hands on aspect where I needed to get my hands dirty. This has lead me to my Love for robotics. Robotics at is core is a mix of software, mechanical and electrical skills. This allows me to get hands on and work on physical projects while at the same time developing quality software and electrical systems.
