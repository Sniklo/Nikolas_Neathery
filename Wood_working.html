Wood Working Project

overview

For this project I went to my local woodmill and baught as many scraps of hard wood that I could get my hands on. I then planced and jointed each piece then glued them together into one block. Once I hace a square like block I choped the corners off so it would be easier to turn. After this I was able to turn it down to a bowl shape. The final step was to rub a food safe oil into the bowl to make the color of each species of wood.
