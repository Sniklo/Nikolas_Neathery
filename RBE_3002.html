RBE: 3002

overview

Following RBE 3001 was the introduction to ROS. The goal of this project was to navigate a maze using A* and SLAM for localization. For this we used turtle bots with lidar. To start, everything was done in SIM then was migrated to a physical robot. This demonstrated that this transition is rarely seamless.

How it works

The first challenge of the project was figuring out how to map the unknown area. We accomplished this by approaching the largest unknown area or frontier and reassessing the area until there were no longer unknown areas or frontiers. Once this was completed the robot was placed in a random location and needed to localize itself then drive to a desired position
